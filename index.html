Posing Packages

Posing is not just a part of stage presentation, but a multifaceted tool in every bodybuilders' arsenal. Posing is much more than a skill used on stage, it is also essential to coaches, helping them track progression through consistent presentation. Posing is an art, and being able to masterfully display the physique you have worked so hard for on stage, is just as important as stepping on stage itself. Showcase your physique with confidence with one of STA's posing packages!

Just The Essentials

Two 45-minute sessions aimed at teaching you how to hit your mandatory poses to your division and organization's specifications.

Stage Ready

Don't just learn how to hit your mandatories, but how to command the stage with your presence to show your strongest points.

Tailored to Fit

This package is for those looking to create a routine but don't know where to start. STA will choreograph, demonstrate, and teach a unique and custom routine for between 30-90 seconds.

No Stone Unturned

STA's most comprehensive posing package. Learn the mandatories, create a stage presence, and develop a routine that is custom tailored to your strongest poses.
